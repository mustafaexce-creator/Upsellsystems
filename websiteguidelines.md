Mix of two premium tech palettes:

| Role | Color | Hex |

### Design Aesthetic
- **Dark, premium, futuristic** — feels like a next-gen tech agency
- **Glassmorphism cards** — subtle glass effect with navy borders
- **Gradient accents** — electric blue to cyan gradients on key elements (buttons, headings, icons)
- **Micro-animations** — smooth fade-ins on scroll, hover glows, number counters
- **Geometric background elements** — subtle grid lines, glowing orbs/particles in background
- **RTL-perfect layout** — every single element must respect Arabic right-to-left reading direction

### Typography (Arabic + Latin fallback)
- **Headlines:** Cairo Bold or Tajawal Black — bold, commanding
- **Body Text:** Tajawal Regular or Noto Sans Arabic — clean and readable
- **Accent Numbers/Stats:** Use a modern Latin font like Space Grotesk for numerics only
- Font must be loaded from Google Fonts (Cairo + Tajawal)
